title: 'Export Leads'
description: 'How to export leads captured from your open houses.'
---

After [viewing leads](/leads/view), you are able to export them to your choice of file format. To get started, press the "Export" button.

## Customize Data

You can customize the data that is included in your export.

Exports will always include the full names of visitors and the associated listing address.

Optionally, you can include the following information:
* Contact Info
* Notes
* Date Visited
* Sign-In Answers

## Export Formats

ListingOpen supports lead exports in Excel (.xlsx), CSV (.csv), JSON (.json), and Text (.txt) file formats.
