## All Leads

You can view all your leads [here](https://listingopen.com/leads).

## Listing-Specific Leads

To view all leads for a specific listing, follow these simple steps:
1. Navigate to the desired listing on the [Listings](https://listingopen.com/listings) page.
2. Click the "Leads" tab.

## Lead Information

Click on any lead to open a new page with all their details, including contact info, notes, and sign-in answers.
