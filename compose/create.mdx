<Tip>**Premium Feature** - Compose requires a Premium plan.</Tip>

To get started with Compose, click [here](http://listingopen.com/compose).

Select the desired preset and enter the required information. Once you're done, select your desired tone, and click "Next".

## Available Presets

ListingOpen offers the following presets:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Create" icon="pencil">
    **Create Listing Description** - Craft magnetic property listings that convert.
    **Create Open House Invite** - Drive open house attendance with enticing copy.
    **Create Neighbourhood Description** - Entice appeal with vivid neighbourhood descriptions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Enhance" icon="magic">
    **Enhance Listing Description** - Elevate property listings with compelling copy.
    **Personalize Listing Description** - Tailor listing descriptions to specific client needs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Highlight" icon="star">
    **Showcase Property Highlights** - Emphasize unique property features.
    **Highlight Property Comparisons** - Streamline comparisons for easier decision-making.
    **Highlight Investment Benefits** - Quantify investment potential for informed decisions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Explain" icon="commenting">
    **Explain Closing Process** - Reduce client stress by explaining the closing process.
    **Explain Industry Jargon** - Ensure clients understand complex industry terms.
    **Explain Lease Agreements** - Foster better tenant-landlord relationships.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
