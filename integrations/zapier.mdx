---
title: 'Zapier'
description: 'Integrate Zapier to create your own ListingOpen-integrated Zap.'
---

Zapier seamlessly connects apps and automates workflows without any coding required.

You can integrate ListingOpen with Zapier effortlessly using these easy steps:
1. Sign in to your Zapier account.
2. Navigate to "Make a Zap" to start creating a new automation workflow.
3. Choose ListingOpen as the trigger app.
4. You can find your ListingOpen API Key in the [Settings](https://app.listingopen.com/settings) page.
4. Follow the prompts to set up triggers and actions based on your preferences.
5. Customize your workflow by adding actions with other apps connected through Zapier.
