---
title: 'Update Listings'
description: 'How to effortlessly update your ListingOpen listings.'
---

To update a listing, follow these simple steps:

1. Visit your [Listings](https://listingopen.com/listings) dashboard.
2. Choose the listing you would like to edit.
3. Navigate between tabs, editing the necessary property details (address, photos, categories, etc.), sign-in rules (questions, follow-up emails, etc.), integrations, and/or design.
4. Make sure to save your changes between edits.

<Note>Make sure to save your changes between edits. If you forget to do so, your listing details will not be updated.</Note>
