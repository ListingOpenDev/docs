---
title: 'Wrapping Up'
description: 'How to end your open house on ListingOpen.'
---

<img src="https://listingopen-photos.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/guides/end_oh_1.gif" />

To end your open house on any device, follow these simple steps:
1. Click the ListingOpen logo in the bottom left corner after [launching your open house](/openhouse/start).
2. Click "End Open House".
3. You can [launch](/openhouse/start) again at any time.
