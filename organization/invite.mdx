---

<Tip>**Premium Feature** - Organizations require a Premium plan.</Tip>

To invite a member to your organization, simply click the "Invite Members" button on your Organization dashboard.

Enter the email they use to sign in to ListingOpen, and choose their organization role, before clicking "Add".

## Organization Roles

**Agents** are able to manage and create their own listings and leads. They cannot access organization-wide settings or view other agents' data.

**Managers** are able to oversee all agents' listings and leads within the organization. They can access agents' data but have no administrative control.

The **Owner** has full control over the organization's listings and leads, and can remove agents and manage organization settings.
