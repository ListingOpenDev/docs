## Editing Member Roles

To edit a member role, simply choose the appropriate new role on the dropdown beside their name. Effects will take place immediately.

For a description of each role's permissions, click [here](/organization/invite).

## Removing Members

To remove a member from your organization, simply click the "Remove" button beside their name. Effects will take place immediately.
