---
title: 'Reset Password'
---

To reset your password, follow these simple steps:

1. Sign in to your ListingOpen account.
2. Visit https://listingopen.com/settings.
3. Select the "Account" tab - this is where you'll find various options related to your account settings.
4. Under the "Actions" section, click on the "Change Password" button.
5. On the new page, enter your new password details.
6. Once done, click on "Save Changes" to confirm and update your password.
