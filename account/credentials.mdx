---
title: 'Integration Credentials'
description: 'On ListingOpen, you can securely store credentials for integrated platforms.'
---

To manage your saved credentials, follow these simple steps:

1. Sign in to your ListingOpen account.
2. Visit https://listingopen.com/settings.
3. Select the "Account" tab - this is where you'll find various options related to your account settings.
4. Under the "Credentials" section, click on the platform you're looking for.
5. On the new page, enter the necessary credentials.
6. Once done, click on "Save & Publish" to store your saved credentials.

When adding new integrations, your saved credentials will now be auto-filled.
